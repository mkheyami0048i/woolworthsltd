Mystery Shopper Opportunity with Woolworths Group Limited

Are you ready to step into the world of mystery shopping and make a real impact on shaping the future of retail excellence? Join us at Woolworths Group Limited as a Mystery Shopper and embark on an exciting journey of discovery!

About the Role:

  • As a Mystery Shopper with Woolworths, you'll have the unique opportunity to visit our branches nationwide and evaluate various aspects of the customer experience. Your role will involve:

  • Visiting Locations: You'll visit Woolworths branches across the country, assessing customer service, cleanliness, product quality, and adherence to company policies.

  • Observation: Using detailed instructions provided, you'll discreetly observe and interact with employees and facilities as a regular customer would.

  • Reporting: After each visit, you'll provide detailed feedback and insights through our reporting system, helping us identify areas for improvement and maintain our high standards.

  • Compensation: Mystery shoppers are typically compensated for their time and may receive reimbursement for any purchases made during the assignment.
    Bonus: Earn up to $150 in reward gift cards.

    How to Apply:

    At Woolworths Group Limited, we offer a dynamic and supportive work environment where excellence is recognized and rewarded. As part of our team, you will have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on the lives of people and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in your field.Join us in shaping the future of retail excellence!
